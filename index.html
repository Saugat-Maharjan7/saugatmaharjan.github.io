    I am a passionate UI/UX Designer holding an Engineering Degree from Kathmandu, Nepal. With more than 3 years of industry experience, I specialize in creating products that offer compelling visual experiences, starting from user research to high-fidelity design. My interest in the design field was sparked during my bachelor's degree.

    I believe that design has the power to transform the way we interact with the world. My focus is on crafting interfaces that are not only intuitive and engaging but also a joy to use. At the core of my design approach is committed to user-centric principles, ensuring that I always prioritize the user's needs and objectives in every design choice I make.

    I am constantly challenging myself to innovate, exploring new design methodologies, and staying up-to-date of the latest trends and technologies in the industry.
