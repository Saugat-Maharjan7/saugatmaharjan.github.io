    Shanti Yoga Ashram - Revamp Website

    User Interface . Responsive Design . Content Creations . Freelance . Part-time .

    Project Overview

    Shanti Yoga Ashram is an organization who aims to preserve and transmit the knowledge from ancient teachings. The revamping of website project aims to transform the yoga ashram into a modern wellness retreat, aligning the traditional practices with contemporary wellness standards. This website seeks to offer an enhanced experience to visitors and residents, promoting holistic well-being through yoga, meditation, healthy living, and spiritual growth.

    The Problem

    The existing website was quite outdated and the organization wanted to have their website look more clean and user-centered, engaging user interface with clear message about them and their services and enhance the user experience with responsive design and align the website design with their brand identity.

    The Objective

    The goal is to attract and engage visitors more effectively with more premium look and feel, streamline navigation for easier content discovery with responsive design. Additionally, the revamped website should showcase about what hey are, what services they provide, provide valuable informative content, and encourage user interaction through clear calls-to-action.

    Design Thinking Process

    The design process was guided by a design thinking approach, which offered a flexible, non-linear framework for problem-solving. This methodology allowed me to empathize with users, define key challenges, ideate creative solutions, and iterate quickly—ultimately leading to a more user-centered and effective design outcome..

    Initial Research

    As part of initial research process, after I first navigated to their old website and had research about how the content can be managed. We had 2 to 3 meetings regarding what contents should be displayed for user-centric design and how the contents can be aligned. We figured out the most important pages that should be more focused. We aligned with a propsed pages and then a sitemap was created upon which the whole website would be build on.

    Summary

    As an conclusion of the research, I found out what the organization wants to make a new revamp website and then worked on page layouts and contents to be placed. On thorough research, a proposed pages were finalized where the contents needed to be placed and modified accordingly.

    Wireframe

    Lets do the rough!

    Started with the wireframes. The wireframes were created with multiple variations for each page for sharing how the website should look and feel. Then final discussion was conducted once wireframes were finished and final design was picked based on their interest of choice.

    Now lets define the final design’s visual aspects

    To establish a consistent and appealing visual identity, I created moodboards and defined the overall visual style for the application. This included designing the logo, selecting a cohesive color palette, and choosing typography that aligned with the brand’s tone and target audience. These foundational elements ensured visual consistency across the entire user interface and helped set the tone for a modern, accessible learning experience.

    Mockup

    After the finalized wireframe with alignment of the contents and proper style guide finalization then final outcome was high-fidelity design.
